UTS Spook-Tacular Halloween Festival 2023

Welcome to the 2023 Spook-Tacular Halloween Festival!




From 12PM to 6PM, Sunday 29th of October!


Get ready for a day of frights, spooks, thrills, and fun cuz it is time for the Spook-Tacular Halloween Festival 2023, one of the biggest event of the year! 🎃🎃🎃‼️


This jampacked event will be featuring 10 societies including: anime@UTS, Drawing Circle, UTS Puzzle Society, K-Pop UTS, Playmakers, Gamers Guild @ UTS, MuscUTS, UTS Pokemon, UTS Maker Society and UTS Literary Society, who will deliver BIG THINGS to the festivals 😈😈😈


These include: Gaming LAN parties, The Minigame Parlor, The Karaoke Bar, Jack O'Lantern 3D Print workshops, Horror Houses, Competitions and so much more.

We have also confirmed 3 live performances from Matcha Overkill, Rollersyd and UTS’s very own MuscUTS, who will be performing on the day


Furthermore, we are conducting a costume competition towards the end of the festival to show off your best Halloween outfits to the crowd!


And don’t forget, there are prizes to be gained on the day from our list of sponsors who are helping to keep this event alive and running


This is a ticketed event. Please make sure to order your tickets via the ActivateUTS website!

That is not all folks, we will also conduct our Spook-Tacular Halloween Festival Costume Competition. From 5:00pm all the way till the end of the festival we host a small scale costume competition where you have the opportunity to show off your costume to everyone in the crowd! There is only one prize (that will be revealed on the day) that you can win and you will be judge by these simple criterias:

How cool/well designed are the costumes (bonus marks if you personally made the costume yourself)
How much cheers were you able to gather from the audience
Are you cosplaying a unique/popular/niche fictional character

Disclaimer: Any costumes that are deemed NSFW (showing a lot of skin) will be immediately disqualified. Also no dangerous weapons/realistic weapons are allow inside of the Halloween Festival.

There are no forms that you need to sign before the festival, just get dressed and rock up to the festival at 4:30pm to sign yourself into the festival. All we will need is your name and you will need to declare whether your costume was made by you or not. I would recommend for participants to dress up in their costumes right before the sign up session has started.

Sign up session time: 4:30pm - 4:45pm

The official costume competition will start at 5:00pm

The competition is predicted to be finished by 6:00pm, the exact time where the Halloween Festival will be finished
