UTS Spook-Tacular Halloween Festival 2023

Welcome to the 2023 Spook-Tacular Halloween Festival!


Get ready for a day of frights, spooks, thrills, and fun cuz it is time for the Spook-Tacular Halloween Festival 2023, one of the biggest event of the year! 🎃🎃🎃‼️


This jampacked event will be featuring 10 societies including: anime@UTS, Drawing Circle, UTS Puzzle Society, K-Pop UTS, Playmakers, Gamers Guild @ UTS, MuscUTS, UTS Pokemon, UTS Maker Society and UTS Literary Society, who will deliver BIG THINGS to the festivals 😈😈😈


These include: Gaming LAN parties, The Minigame Parlor, The Karaoke Bar, Jack O'Lantern 3D Print workshops, Horror Houses, Competitions and so much more.

We have also confirmed 3 live performances from Matcha Overkill, Rollersyd and UTS’s very own MuscUTS, who will be performing on the day


Furthermore, we are conducting a costume competition towards the end of the festival to show off your best Halloween outfits to the crowd!


And don’t forget, there are prizes to be gained on the day from our list of sponsors who are helping to keep this event alive and running


This is a ticketed event. Please make sure to order your tickets via the ActivateUTS website!
